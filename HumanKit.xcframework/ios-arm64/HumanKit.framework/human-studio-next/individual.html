Plans and Pricing

Can I use the BioDigital Human for free?
Yes! We offer a free Personal plan for individuals and a 14-day free trial for businesses and schoolsâ€” no credit card required. Sign up here.
What is the difference between your Personal and Personal Plus plans?
Our Personal plan includes 10 Page Views and 5 Saved Models per month while our Personal Plus plan gives you access to unlimited Page Views and Saved Models.
How do you define a Creator?
A Creator is a user with rights to author 3D Models in Human Studio and save them to My Library.
Can I switch my Personal plan at any time?
If you have a Personal plan, you can upgrade to Personal Plus or start a free School or Business trial at any time on the Account page under Subscription. Upgrading gives you immediate access to the features included in your chosen plan.

Already subscribed to our Personal Plus plan? Your plan will automatically renew each year unless you cancel it, and you can cancel at any time. Click here for instructions.

Billing & Payment

What types of payment do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards and PayPal. We are unable to issue invoices or receipts for Individual plans.
How often will I be billed?
Our Personal Plus plan is billed annually.
How do I cancel my Personal Plus plan?
Our Personal Plus plan automatically renews at the end of each billing cycle. You can cancel at any time prior to your billing cycle start date by going to Account and going to the Subscription section. Find more information here.
What is your refund policy?
Payments for our Personal Plus plan are non-refundable. Read our refund policy here.
