Our Class plan is best for educators using the BioDigital Human for one class or a small number of students, and our Institution plan is designed for use across multiple classes or faculty.
What are Page Views?
A Page View is registered when the Human Viewer starts loading when embedded within your LMS, website, or application, or accessed via a standalone link.

Free Trials

How do your free trials work?
We offer a 14-day free trial for schools that gives you access to all features in our Class plan, including:
  • Access to over 700 anatomy, physiology, health condition and treatment models on our web and mobile apps
  • Unlimited Saved Models
  • A collaborative workspace for sharing models with other members of your team
  • Human Studio, our suite of authoring tools
  • Quiz Builder, which enables you to create interactive, virtual quizzes
  • The ability to publish your saved models as a link or embed code for use in your LMS, website, or application
Click here to start your free trial. If you already have a Personal account, log in to your account and navigate to the Team page.
What happens at the end of my free trial?
At the end of your 14-day free trial, you will have two options:
  • Contct us and we will help you choose the plan that is right for you.
  • Let your trial expire, downgrading your account to our free Personal plan. Note that your saved models and team configuration will remain intact, so you can still upgrade to a paid plan later with no loss of content, team members, or settings. Learn more here.
